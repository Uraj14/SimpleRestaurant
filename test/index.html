Momos

There are typically two types of momo, steamed and fried. Momo is usually served with a dipping sauce (locally called chutney/achhar[5]), normally made with tomato as the base ingredient. Soup momo is a dish with steamed momo immersed in a meat broth. Pan-fried momo is also known as kothey momo. Steamed momo served in hot sauce is called C-momo. There are also a variety of Tibetan momos, including tingmo and thaipo.

Paneer Tikka Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a traditional Punjabi recipe in which soft pieces of Paneer are cooked in rich creamy butter & tomato gravy. Paneer Butter Masala goes by various names like Butter Paneer, Paneer Makhani etc. the dish is characterised by rich and aromatic gravy made by using tomato puree, onion paste, cashew paste, and some essential Indian spices.
